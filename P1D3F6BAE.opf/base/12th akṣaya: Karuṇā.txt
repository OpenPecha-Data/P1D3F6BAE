pa’i raṅ bźin sems can thams cad la phan ’dogs par byed pa, dṅos pa gaṅ la yaṅ mi chags
mi gnas pa ste; tshul khrims de la sems can rnams la ’god ciṅ, tshul khrims de sems can rnams
kyis thob par bya’o, źes dmigs pa’i rgyu las byuṅ bas na sñiṅ rje chen po ni tshul khrims
’chal pa’i sems can rnams la lta ba ste, sems can ’chal pa’i tshul khrims can rnams sruṅ
bar byed pa’o. rgyu daṅ raṅ bźin bśad zin to.

las bstan pa’i phyir btsun pa śa ra dva ti’i bu, de ltar byaṅ chub sems dpa’i ṅes par ’byuṅ
ba gaṅ ci yaṅ ruṅ ste źes bya ba la sogs pa gsuṅs te, byaṅ chub sems dpa’ rnams kyi ñon moṅs
pa’am khams gsum las ṅes par ’byuṅ ba’i tshogs gaṅ yaṅ ruṅ ste, de dag thams cad sñiṅ rje
chen po las skye’o. de ltar sñiṅ rjes skyed par byed pas na, ’di ltar bya ba byed pa’i
phyir sñiṅ rje chen po źes bya’o.gaṅ byed ce na? ’di ltar sbyin par byed ces bya ba la sogs
pa gsuṅs te, gźan ni go sla bas ma bśad do.gdon mi za bar byed ces bya ba ni phyin ci ma
log pa’am, chud mi za bar byed ces bya ba’i don to.sems can thams cad kyi bya ba ji sñed
yod ces so sor ñe bar gnas źes bya ba ni sems can thams cad kyi las su bya ba daṅ, phan pa
daṅ, bde bar bya ba ci yod ces rtag tu rtogs par byed pa gnas źes bya ba’i don to.

sñiṅ rje mi zad pa bśad zin to.
