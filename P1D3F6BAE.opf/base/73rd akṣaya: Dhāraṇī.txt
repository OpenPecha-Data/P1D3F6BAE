thos pa mi brjed pa gaṅ źe na? gaṅ gzuṅs so sor thob pa ste, de’i dbaṅ du byas nas de la gzuṅs
ni mi zad pa gaṅ źe na źes bya ba la sogs pa gsuṅs so – "Who is the one with no forgetfulness
concerning what he has heard (śrutāsaṃpramoṣa)? The one who has attained memory (dhāraṇīpratilabdha).
With reference to him (tam adhipatiṃ kṛtvā), it is said ...de yaṅ 1) gaṅ
gis mi brjed pa daṅ, 2) gaṅ mi brjed pa daṅ, 3) mi brjed pa ñams pa med pa’o. de la 1) gaṅ
gis mi brjed pa ni dge ba’i rtsa ba’i tshogs bsags pa’i dran pa źes bya nas bstan to; 2)
gaṅ mi brjed pa ni chos kyi phuṅ po brgyad khri bźi stoṅ źes bya ba nas ’di ni gzuṅs źes
bya’o źes bya ba’i bar gyis bstan to; 3) mi brjed pa ñams par mi ’gyur ba ni gaṅ gi tshe
bskal pa gźan gyi rjes źes bya ba nas brtsams te, skyu ru ra’i ’bru ’dug pa bźin du mthoṅ
źes bya ba yan chad kyis bstan to.

sṅon gyi dge ba’i rtsa ba’i tshogs bsags pa’i dran pa źes bya ba ni tshe rabs sṅa ma’i dus nas
bsod nams daṅ ye śes kyi tshogs bsags pa’i rgyu las byuṅ ba’i dran pas so. ’dzin pa ni
yi ge rnams so, kun ’dzin pa ni don rnams so, mi brjed pa ni phyis kyaṅ yi ge mi brjed par
byed pa’o, dran pas yaṅ dag par ’dzin pa ni phyis kyaṅ don mi brjed par byed pa ste; de dag
gis ni gzuṅs kyi gsuṅ rab ’jig rten du bśad de gźag pa rnams mi brjed par byed pa bstan
to.saṅs rgyas thams cad kyis gsuṅs pa źes bya ba la sogs pa ni ’phral du gsuṅs pa’i don daṅ
tshig mi brjed par byed pa bstan to; chud za bar mi ’gyur ba ni chos daṅ don dran pa’i
ye śes de med par mi ’gyur ba’o.brjed par yaṅ mi ’gyur ba ni kha cig dran pa kha cig mi dran
pa ste, dran pa dri ma can du ma gyur pa’o.’phags pa’i śes rab kyi mig gis źes bya ba ni
zag pa med pa’i ye śes kyi mig daṅ ’dra bas źes bya ba’i don to.
