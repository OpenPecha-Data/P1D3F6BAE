– "To teach what [the bodhisattva] facing intentions (āśayābhimukha) is ...".de mi zad pa
yaṅ: sṅon gyi dus ’di tshun chad dran no, źes dran pa’i mtha’ thug pa med pa ni mi zad pa’am,
yaṅ na bdag gi sṅon gnas dran pa ’ba’ źig ma yin gyi, sems can thams cad kyi sṅon gyi
gnas kyaṅ dran pas sems can mi zad pa’i phyir de dag gi sṅon gyi gnas dran pa yaṅ mi zad pa’o.
rnam pa gcig tu dran pa de ni chos kyi dbyiṅs kyi gnas kyis byin gyis brlabs pa’o źes
bya bas kyaṅ mi zad pa bstan te, chos kyi dbyiṅs rnam par dag pa ni mtshan ma daṅ dṅos po
thams cad las ’das pas mi zad pa yin te, des byin gyis brlabs pa yaṅ de daṅ mthun pa’am,
de’i raṅ bźin rtog pas, de lta bu’i dran pa yaṅ mtha’ med pa’i phyir mi zad pa’o. de yaṅ rnam
pa bźir rig par bya ste, 1) gaṅ gis dran pa daṅ, 2) gaṅ dran pa daṅ, 3) ji ltar dran pa
daṅ, 4) dran nas don chen por ’gyur ba’o – "It is imperishable (akṣaya): ’One remembers
earlier times up till now’, thus the limit (anta) of remembrance is not found (anavastha)
and thus it is imperishable (akṣaya). Or, again, as one remembers the earlier lives of all
beings and not only those of oneself, since the beings are imperishable (akṣaya), remembering
their earlier lives is also imperishable (akṣaya). In short, also by "That remembrance
is sustained by the sphere of all moments of existence (sā smṛtiḥ svadhiṣṭhitā dharmadhātusthityā)"
which is taught [infra], it is imperishable (akṣaya): the pure sphere of all moments
of existence (viśuddhadharmadhātu) transcends all distinguishing marks and existing
things (sarvanimittabhāvātikrānta) and is thus imperishhable (akṣaya); to be supported by
that [sphere] is to be in accordance with it, or, to discern its essence (svabhāva), and
thus that remembrance (smṛti) is imperishable (akṣaya) as it is endless (ananta). This should
be known through four forms: ... 1) de dran pa des sṅon gyi gnas rjes su dran te źes bya
ba nas brtsams te, pha rol tu phyin pa thams cad kyis tshogs bsags pa’o źes bya ba yan
chad kyis gaṅ gis dran pa bstan to, 2) de dran pa des sṅon gyi gnas rjes su dran te źes bya
ba nas brtsams te, bskal pa brgya stoṅ phrag du ma yaṅ dran źes bya ba yan chad kyis gaṅ
dran pa bstan to, 3) sems can miṅ che ge mo źes bya ba nas brtsams te, des bdag daṅ sems
can gźan gyi sṅon gyi mtha’ nas bzuṅ ste, sṅon gyi gnas rjes su dran źes bya ba yan chad kyis
ji ltar dran pa bstan to, 4) de bdag gi dge ba’i rtsa ba źes bya ba nas brtsams te, thams
cad mkhyen pa’i phyir bsṅo ste źes bya ba yan chad kyis don chen por ’gyur ba bstan to.

de źes bya ba ni byaṅ chub sems dpa’ de źes bya ba’i tha tshig go, des źes bya ba ni sṅon gyi
gnas rjes su dran pa des źes bya ba’i don to.dran pa de ni chos kyi dbyiṅs kyi gnas kyis
byin gyis brlab pa’o źes bya ba la gzuṅ ba daṅ ’dzin pa thams cad las ’das pa’i chos kyi
dbyiṅs ñid saṅs rgyas kyi chos thams cad skye ba’i rgyu yin pas chos kyi dbyiṅs kyi gnas te,
dran pa de’i chos kyi gnas de’i rgyu las skyes pa’am, chos kyi dbyiṅs daṅ raṅ bźin mthun
pa’i phyir byin gyis brlabs pa źes bya’o.rab tu rig pas na mi g-yo ba’o źes bya ba ni dran
pa des chos thams cad phyin ci ma log par śin tu rtog pa na ’khrul pa’am ’gyur ba med ces
bya ba’i don to.ye śes śin tu sbyaṅ ba byas pas na mi rgod pa’o źes bya ba ni śes śiṅ dran
par byas pa’i dṅos po la rab tu byaṅ bar gyur pas na ’phyar ba daṅ rgod pa med ces bya
ba’i tha tshig go.źi gnas la rab tu gnas pas na gtse ba med ces bya ba ni tiṅ ṅe ’dzin sems
rtse gcig pa’i mtshan ñid la legs par ’dug pas tiṅ ṅe ’dzin daṅ mi mthun pa’i phyogs phyin
ci log tu rtog ciṅ dpyod pa la sogs pa’i skyon med ces bya ba’i don to.lhag mthoṅ gis
śin tu zin pas na nor ba med ces bya ba la lhag mthoṅ ni śes rab la bya ste, dran par bya
ba’i dṅos po rnams śes rab kyis ma nor bar zin ciṅ rtog pas na ma ’khrul pa’o.This item is
omitted in ṭ.dran źiṅ mi brjed pa’i chos can pas na źes bya ba la sṅon thos pa’i chos daṅ
don rnams ma brjed pa ni dran pa’o, phyis brjed par mi ’gyur ba ni mi brjed pa ste, de lta
bu’i chos daṅ ldan pas sṅon gyi gnas dran pa’i mṅon par śes pa dran par bya’o.theg pa chen
po rab tu śes pa’i phyir źes bya ba ni dran pa des theg pa chen po’i chos khoṅ du chud
pa’i phyir khoṅ du chud pa’i rgyu las byuṅ ba’i bsod nams kyi tshogs daṅ ldan źes bya ba’i
don to.gźan la rag ma lus pas na ye śes kyi tshogs bsags pa’o źes bya ba ni bsam gtan ’khor
gsum yoṅs su dag pa gźan gyi driṅ la mi ’jog pas ye śes kyi tshogs bsags pa’o.thams cad
kyi pha rol tu ’gro ba’i phyir źes bya ba la pha rol ces bya ba ni mthar phyin pa’am mchog
tu phyin pa la bya ste, pha rol tu phyin pa thams cad kyi mchog gam, mthar phyin pa’i phyir
pha rol tu phyin pa thams cad kyi tshogs bsags pa’o.

tshe rabs gcig kyaṅ dran no źes bya ba ni skye ba gcig kyaṅ dran no źes bya ba’i tha tshig go.
gźan ni go sla bas ma bśad de, gaṅ dran pa yaṅ bśad zin to.

bdag gi miṅ ni ’di źes bya ba la sogs pa la tshe rabs gaṅ du skyes pa’i tshe re re’i miṅ yaṅ
dran pa ste, dper na tshe ’di’i tshe na ni miṅ yaṅ ’di źes bya, tshe ’di’i tshe na ni miṅ
’di źes bya ba dran pa’o; rigs ’di źes bya ba ni tshe rabs de dag tu skyes pas na bdag rigs
bźi las gaṅ daṅ gaṅ du skyes pa dran pa’o; rus źes bya ba ni rigs bźi las kyaṅ mthon dman
du phye ba śes pas te, tshe rabs de dag na bdag gi pha’i rus ni ’di źes bya, ma’i rus ni
’di źes bya źes bya ba la sogs pa’o; kha dog ni ’di ’dra źes bya ba ni kha dog yid du ’oṅ
ba daṅ, yid du mi ’oṅ ba daṅ, mdzes pa daṅ mi mdzes pa ci ’dra bar gyur pa daṅ dran pa’o;
kha zas ni ’di lta bu źig za za źes bya ba ni ston pa dran pa ste, tshe rabs de daṅ de dag
tu kha zas ci daṅ ji lta bu zos pa dran pa’o; tshe’i tshad ni ’di tsam źig thub ces bya
ba ni tshe’i ’du byed dran pa ste, tshe rabs de dag tu tshe’i tshad ji srid gnas gnas pa
las tshe zad de ni lan du śi, ye ’drog gis ni lan du bsad pa dran pa’o; bde ba daṅ sdug bsṅal
myoṅ ba źes bya bas ni dar ba daṅ rgud pa dran pa bstan te, tshe rabs de daṅ de dag tu
dar ciṅ bde ba lan maṅ du myoṅ ba daṅ, rgud ciṅ sdug bsṅal ba lan maṅ du myoṅ ba so sor dran
pa’o; ji ltar bdag gis dran pa de bźin du sems can gźan gyis yaṅ dran par sbyar ro.

rnam pa daṅ bcas źes bya ba la rnam pa ni tshe rabs gcig nas bskal pa bye ba khrag khrig maṅ
po dran pa’o; rgyu mtshan ni dus daṅ rus la sogs pa gaṅ yin pa dran pa’o; yul phyogs ni bdag
de nas śi ’phos nas che ge mo źig tu skyes źes dran pa’o. ji ltar dran pa bśad zin to.

sṅon gyi rgyu las byuṅ ba dran źes bya ba ni dge ba’i rtsa ba sṅon ci daṅ ci bsags pa dran pa’o;
byaṅ chub tu yoṅs su sṅo bar byed do źes bya ba ni dge ba’i rtsa ba’i rgyu yod par dran
źiṅ śes nas dge ba’i rtsa ba de dag byaṅ chub tu bsṅo bar byed pa’o.sṅon gyi ’du byed kyi
sdug bsṅal źes bya ba ni bdag sṅon ’gyur ba’i sdug bsṅal gyis sdug bsṅal bar gyur pa’am,
’du byed kyis sdug bsṅal bar gyur pa de dag dran nas źes bya ba’i tha tshig go; rton par
byed do źes bya ba ni ’du byed rnams mi rtag pa la sogs par lta’o źes bya ba’i don to.gzugs
kyis dregs pa med pa yin źes bya ba ni de ltar mi rtag par śes pas bdag ni gzugs bzaṅ po
mdzes pa’o sñam pa’i rlom sems med pa ste, mdzes pa de yaṅ de ma thag tu med par ’gyur bar
khoṅ du chud pa’i phyir te, kha dog gis dregs pa med pa la sogs pa la yaṅ ci rigs par sbyar
ro.skye ba thams cad ’dod pa ma yin źes bya ba ni skye ba’i gnas thams cad du skye bar
’dod par mi skye źes bya ba’i don to.bsams bźin du srid par skye ba ’dzin źes bya ba ni
sems can gyi don du śes bźin du khams gsum du skye bar byed pa’osṅon gyi kun nas ñon moṅs
pa’i spyod pas ṅo tsha źiṅ ’dzem ste źes bya ba ni chos thams cad mi rtag pa’i mtshan ñid
du śes pas, de lta bu dag gi phyir sṅon sdig pa byas pa ni mi rigs pa byas so źes ṅo tsha
źiṅ ’dzem pa skye ba’o.rgyas par byed do źes bya ba ni dge ba’i rtsa ba bsags pa byaṅ chub
tu bsṅos nas ’phel źiṅ rgya chen por byed pa’o.des mi mthun par yoṅs su bsṅo ba spaṅs te
źes bya ba ni dge ba’i rtsa ba ci bsags pa daṅ, ñan thos sam, raṅ saṅs rgyas sam, lha’am,
mir skye ba mi bsṅo’i, dkon mchog gsum gyi rigs rgyun mi chad pa daṅ, thams cad mkhyen pa’i
ye śes thob par bya ba’i phyir bsṅo’o. ’di’i don chen por ’gyur ro.
