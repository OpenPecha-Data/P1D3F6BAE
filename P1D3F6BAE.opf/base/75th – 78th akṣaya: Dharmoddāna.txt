the essential character of all conditioned things (sarvasaṃskāralakṣaṇajña), 2) knowing
the essential character of all sullied things (sarvasāsravalakṣaṇajña), 3) knowing the
essential character of all moments of existence (sarvadharmalakṣaṇajña), and 4) free of conceit
caused by peace (śāntinirmāṇa); with reference to him (tam adhipatiṃ kṛtvā) it is said:
...; as a summarizing title (piṇḍārtha) it is taught as Defining the Attainment of That
(tatprāptivyavasthāpanā). They are called Summaries of Religion (dharmoddāna) since they
teach these four statements concerned with the impermanence, etc., of all conditioned (saṃskṛta)
and unconditioned (asaṃskṛta) moments of existence in a condensed way (samāsanirdeśatas).mi

mi rtag pa’i don yaṅ rnam pa gñis te, med pa’i don daṅ ’jig pa’i don to. ’dir ni chos thams
cad stoṅ pa yin par dgoṅs pas med pa’i don la mi rtag pa’i don du dgoṅs kyi, ’jig pa’i don
du ma yin te, de bas na med pa’i don daṅ mi chags pa’i don te dṅos po rnam par ’jig pa’i
don ni ma yin no źes gsuṅs so. "The content of the word ‘non-existence’ is the content of
the word ‘impermanence’" etc.de la med pa’i don ni don dam par gzuṅ ba daṅ ’dzin pa kun brtags
kyi chos gaṅ yaṅ rtag tu yod pa ma yin pa’i phyir med pa ste, de ni mi rtag pa’i don

po yod pa las źig ste med par ’gyur ba las mi rtag ces bya ba ma yin gyi, ye nas raṅ bźin

’jig pa med ciṅ rtag tu stoṅ pa de ni mi rtag pa’i don to.

zag pa daṅ bcas pa thams cad kyi mtshan ñid śes pa bstan pa’i dbaṅ du byas nas de la sdug bsṅal

the one who knows the essential character of all sullied things (sarvasāsravalakṣaṇajñanirdeśam
adhipatiṃ kṛtvā) it is said ...sdug bsṅal gyi don kyaṅ rnam pa lṅa ste, 1) dga’ ba las
bzlog pa’i don daṅ, 2) dga’ ba daṅ bral ba’i don daṅ, 3) dga’ ba’i yul ma yin pa’i don
daṅ, 4) sgra tsam du zad pa’i don daṅ, 5) raṅ bźin gyis stoṅ pa’i don to.

de la 1) dga’ ba med pa’i don ni sdug bsṅal gyi don to źes bya bas dga’ ba las bzlog pa’i don
rnam par bstan te, sdug bsṅal ni dga’ ba las bzlog pa ste, gaṅ dga’ ba’am chags par ’gyur
ba’i dṅos po med ciṅ de las bzlog pa’i don ni sdug bsṅal gyi don to.2) dga’ ba zad pa’i
don ni sdug bsṅal gyi don to źes bya bas dga’ ba daṅ bral ba’i don bstan te, dga’ ba’i yul
daṅ bral ba ni sdug bsṅal skye bas dga’ ba daṅ chags pa rnams daṅ bral ba’i don ni sdug bsṅal
gyi don to.3) smon pa med pa’i don ni sdug bsṅal gyi don to źes bya ba dga’ ba’i yul

sdug bsṅal gyi don to.4) sdug bsṅal gyi sgra rab tu bstan pa źes bya bas sgra tsam du zad
